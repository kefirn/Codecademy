Our projects

Green building

The 256-foot and 344-foot towers are covered head to toe in more than 700 trees and 90 species of plants.

All the plant life helps reduce smog, dampen noise levels, produce oxygen, and regulate temperatures inside the two buildings.

During the winter, sunlight can easily pass through the bare plant life and help heat people's rooms. During the summer, the leaves can block harsh rays from making apartments too hot.

Inside the building, a complex irrigation system redirects the water people use back onto the porches to sustain plant life.

Oil refinery

The oil refinery consist of a new crude oil processing facility with a big processing capacity of crude oil per day.

The refinery facility include a crude distillation unit (CDU), a vacuum distillation unit (VDU), a continuous catalytic reforming (CCR) unit, an isomerization unit, a naphtha hydro-treating (NHT) unit, a hydrodesulfurization (HDS) unit, a hydrocracker unit (HCU), and a solvent deasphalting (SDA) unit.

The oil refinery also include an amine recovery unit, a sulphur recovery unit, and a wastewater treatment plant.

Water treatment

Rehabilitation & Upgrade of Water Treatment Plant.

Drinking water is taken from the 10 km away river, by treatment plant and pumped to the town. The plant needed upgrade to match the expanding population.

New plant include the intake, new pre-treatment, clarifier, sand filter and new storage tank with high pressure pump station.
